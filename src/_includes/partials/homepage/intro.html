Rare Gem Talent School

Rare-gem Talent school was conceived in 2012, through the joint efforts of Nancy Munyi and Phyllis Kariuki. The name Rare-gem means a gem you do not get anywhere. The directors took the challenge of starting a school positively since the need was quite evident. This put them in the face of daunting challenges such as the lack of land, resources and premises on which to build a school. This saw them start the school in a rented three bedroomed house in a student hostile environment.

In addition to this, the lack of financial resources put them on the receiving end of anger and distrust by trained teachers who expected a high salary respective of the size of the school. It was quite a headache how to allocate the merger funds collected from fees in a manner that the rent, food, books, salaries and other expenses covered. However difficult it turned to be; the directors were motivated to stick to the course for they believed that they could positively impact the lives of children diagnosed with Dyslexia.

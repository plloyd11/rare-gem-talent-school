The next phase

A growing institution

At the end of the 1 st year since the school was opened, it boasted of a population of 10 students. In addition to this, the school relocated to a more student friendly environment thus boosting the teachers as well as student morale. Through unending patience and consistency, the school management enlightened parents of children born with dyslexia that this was and is a lifelong condition that can be managed through positive teaching, understanding and encouraging learners to never cease to strive their best.

In 2015, 3 years since its conception, Rare gem was the home to a total of 62 students. The school became more inclusive and open to students who had different types of learning difficulties. Through its trained teachers and highly motivated directors, Rare gem created an Individual Education Program (IEP) that was tailor made to the various learning difficulties experienced in its students.

Many children with Specific Learning Difficulties are made to believe that failure in school is okay. Rare Gem Talent School believes and shares a different mindset. Dyslexia can be a gift, not a hindrance. Taught in a conducive environment and access to trained teachers, dyslexic children can and will succeed academically. We have the opportunity to uncover their unique talents and strengths. The board of directors and teachers at the Rare Gem Talent School are geared to make a difference in the lives of children with dyslexia, one student, two students, and eventually a whole society.

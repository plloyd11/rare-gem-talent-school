Goals

  • To raise the funds needed to build a world-class boarding school in Kenya that will optimize the abilities of diverse learners so they can achieve their fullest potential.
  • To facilitate seminars, workshops and teacher trainings throughout Kenya that will empower educators, policymaking and community members with the skills, and awareness, to recognize the signs of Dyslexia, and maximize Dyslexic people’s performance in school and in the workplace.
  • To build a strong working relationship with Kenya’s Ministry of Education, education institutions, academic researchers, local support groups, education practitioners as well as Dyslexia organisations from other countries in order to create a national standardised accommodation system for all of Kenya’s neurodiverse students.

Mission

To build a world-class academic centre dedicated to helping Dyslexic students in Kenya, enriching local community members by increasing their awareness of Dyslexia, and building an international community for the purposes of sharing information.

Vision

In partnership with education authorities (Ministry of Education), teachers and medical professionals we will establish an educational accommodation system throughout Kenya that will create equity and opportunity for neurodiverse children to receive an education from Nursery/Pre-primary to the highest levels of education.

As of the start of the 2020 academic year, 201 students were registered in Rare gem Talent School. This growth proves the commendable work and need that the school serves. All signs show that that this growth is expected to continue as the school adopts more effective teaching methods for children with learning difficulties. As of now, Rare gem has a single stream in each class; from baby class till high school. In addition, it plans to expand its existing premises and renovate the computer laboratory and therapy room where learners who require sensory integration are attended.

The educators at Rare Gem, through unending patience and consistency, continue to enlighten parents of children born with Dyslexia. We teach that Dyslexia is a lifelong condition that can become a gift through positive teaching, understanding and encouraging learners to never cease striving for their best.

Many children with Specific Learning Disabilities are made to believe that failure in school is their only option. The Rare Gem Talent School believes in and shares a different mindset. Dyslexia can be a gift, not a hindrance. Taught in a conducive environment and with access to trained teachers, Dyslexic children can and will thrive academically. We have the opportunity to uncover their unique talents and strengths while providing them with opportunities for a bright future. The board of directors and teachers at the Rare Gem Talent School are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of children with Dyslexia - One student, two students, and eventually the whole society.
